    • Ministries

    At Insight Christian Assembly, we believe in nurturing faith at every stage of life. Explore our ministries and find your place to grow and serve.

    Youth Ministry

    Our youth ministry provides a safe and engaging environment for teenagers to grow in their faith, build lasting friendships, and discover their God-given purpose through worship, Bible study, and fun activities.

    Children's Ministry

    We provide age-appropriate teaching and activities to help children develop a strong foundation in their relationship with God. Our dedicated team creates a fun, safe environment where kids can learn and grow.

    Women's Ministry

    Our women's ministry offers fellowship, Bible study, and support for women of all ages and stages of life. Join us as we encourage one another and grow deeper in our walk with Christ.

    Men's Ministry

    Men gather for fellowship, accountability, and spiritual growth through various activities and Bible studies. We build strong men of faith who lead with integrity in their homes and communities.

    Worship Ministry

    Our worship team leads the congregation in praise and worship, creating an atmosphere for encountering God's presence. If you have musical gifts, we'd love to have you join us.

    Outreach Ministry

    We are committed to serving our community and spreading the gospel through various outreach programs and initiatives. Together, we make a difference in the lives of those around us.
