    Our Vision

    At Insight Christian Assembly, we are committed to expanding our facilities to better serve our growing congregation and community. Your generous contribution to our Building Fund helps us create a space where more people can experience the love of God, grow in their faith, and find community.

    ICA Nigeria

    Building fund for our Nigeria congregation

    Your generosity helps build a place of worship for our brothers and sisters in Nigeria.

    ICA Benin City Temple Project

    We are building a new temple in Benin City, Nigeria to serve our growing congregation in Africa. This project will provide a permanent place of worship for hundreds of believers and will serve as a beacon of hope in the community.

    Total Project Cost

    ₦177,666,996

    Approximately $110,000 USD

    Project Breakdown

    Foundation & Structure

    • 14,000 blocks for construction
    • 420 tonnes of granite
    • 1,450 bags of cement
    • Iron rod reinforcements

    Roofing & Ceiling

    • Metal roofing with long span aluminum
    • 1,200 sq meters of POP/suspended ceiling

    Interior Finishing

    • 1,200 meters of floor tiles
    • Windows and burglary protection
    • Interior and exterior painting

    Utilities & Facilities

    • Complete electrical wiring
    • Plumbing for toilets and facilities
    • 22 doors including security doors
    • Children's church area
